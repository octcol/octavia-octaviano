About

Octavia Octaviano is a writer and cultural theorist based in the Pacific Northwest. Her work exists at the intersection of social commentary and personal mysticism, exploring how the large structures of society imprint themselves on the quiet moments of our daily lives.

With a background in continental philosophy and a practice rooted in contemplative traditions, Octavia seeks to dismantle the noise of the modern era to find the signal underneath. She writes extensively on conscious parenting, the ethics of memory, and the lost art of solitude.

When she isn't writing, she is usually observing the rain, reading three books simultaneously, or negotiating with her kids on various things.
